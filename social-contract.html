Social Contract

Creating safe and inclusive gaming spaces for everyone

Our Commitment

At Ohr Games, we believe that tabletop gaming should be a safe, inclusive, and enjoyable experience for everyone. This social contract outlines our commitment to fostering respectful and welcoming gaming communities.

Core Principles

Respect

All players, regardless of background, identity, or experience level, deserve to be treated with dignity and respect.

Inclusion

We welcome players of all backgrounds, identities, and skill levels to our tables and communities.

Safety

Everyone has the right to feel safe and comfortable while playing our games.

Community Guidelines

Communication

Use inclusive language and avoid discriminatory comments. Be patient with new players and those learning the rules.

Content Warnings

Discuss potentially sensitive content before sessions. Use safety tools like X-cards and lines/veils to ensure everyone's comfort.

Conflict Resolution

Address conflicts respectfully and directly. When in doubt, prioritize the comfort and safety of all players.

Enforcement

Violations of this social contract may result in removal from games, communities, or events. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone who creates an unsafe or unwelcoming environment for others.
